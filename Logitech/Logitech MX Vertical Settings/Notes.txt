Driver name: Logitech Options

This mouse easily wakes up the computer for some reason. To disable this mouse from waking up the computer, go to Device Manager > for every item under "Mice and other pointing devices," do the following: right-click > Properties > Power Management tab > uncheck Allow this device to wake the computer

There may also be items under "Keyboards" that you have to disable the ability to wake the computer for..
