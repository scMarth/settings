Switch from 1.03 to Slippi:
    right-click game > Gecko codes > check:
        Required: General Codes
        Required: Slippi Recording
        Required: Slippi Online

    config > Gamecube > device settings > Slot A = Slippi

Switch from Slippi to 1.03:
    right-click game > Gecko codes > uncheck everything

    config > Gamecube > device settings > Slot A = GCI Folder
