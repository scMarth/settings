Pair: Hold Share + PS button

Control Panel > Devices and Printers > Right-cick Wireless Controller > Properties > Hardware > for every item:
   click 'Properties' > Change settings > Power Management (if it has it) > Uncheck "Allow the computer to turn off this device to save power"

There are also controller settings in Steam for turning off the controller that should be disabled.
