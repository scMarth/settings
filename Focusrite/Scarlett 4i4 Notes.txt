Download Focusrite Control for this device
Plug in USB-C to PC / mic / headphones > reset to default in Focusrite Control then you should be able to hear your mic from the headphones

If you are recording on OBS and you are only getting audio from one side (either only left or only right), right-click the sound in the bottom right in Windows > sound settings > for input, select analogue 1 + 2 then press the '>' > for input settings format, select 1 channel (studio quality)
